Hi there!

I'm Patricio, information systems engineer and senior software developer. I love open data, civic technologies, and I'm openly against the Silicon Valley culture of exponential growth and 10x engineers.

When not in the computer I'm usually relaxing at home, hiking or taking some pictures.

The modern web is completely over-engineered, a dumb race to create and use over-engineered tools and frameworks to solve simple tasks, like a blog. I want this to be a place where I don't feel overwhelmed by thousands of injected lines of code.

The goal of this website is to have a showcase of work and ideas, easy to share and to update. Something minimal that I can evolve in time and it is not tide to a specifict tool.
